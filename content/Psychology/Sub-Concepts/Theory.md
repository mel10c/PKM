##### Theory
> ------------------------------------------------------------
> A rational type of abstract thinking about a phenomenon, or the results of such thinking
>
> ------------------------------------------------------------

- Why something will happen 
- Should account for existing data 
- Predictions about future data (hypotheses) 
- Explanative 
- **Falsifiable**
