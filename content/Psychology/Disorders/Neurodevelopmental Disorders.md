##### Neurodevelopmental Disorders
> ------------------------------------------------------------
> A group of disorders that affect the development of the [[nervous system]], leading to [[abnormal]] [[brain]] function which may affect [[Emotion]], [[learning]] ability, self-control, and [[memory]]
>
> ------------------------------------------------------------
